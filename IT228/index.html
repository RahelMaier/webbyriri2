Our Team

Mary Love

Owner/Hair Designer

She specializes in lived-in color, blondes, balayage and textured haircuts. Mary enjoys working one-on-one with each guest to determine the style and color is not only preferred but will work best with their day-to-day lifestyle. Through this process she works to educate her guests on her styling techniques so each guest can recreate the style at home. Combining all of this helps to customize a look that will showcase a client at their most beautiful and self-confident.

Kat Mark

Co-Owner/ operator

Kat is the Co-Owner/operator of Sensa Salon. Kat comes from a family of stylists and grew up in the industry. Kat started her career in Atlanta and later moved to Arizona. She and her husband decided to relocate to Seattle, and now call Seattle home for good. Kat excels at color, color correction and balayage, but she loves doing anything that involves hair, no matter how short, long, wavy, or curly. Regardless of what you’re looking for, Kat has you covered.
